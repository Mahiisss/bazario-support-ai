DOCUMENT: Bazario Disputes Policy
VERSION: 1.8
LAST UPDATED: March 2026

SECTION 1: TYPES OF DISPUTES

Bazario handles the following dispute categories:

- Damaged item received
- Incorrect item received
- Missing items from order
- Item significantly not as described
- Order marked delivered but not received
- Unauthorized or fraudulent transaction
- Marketplace seller disputes

Each category has specific resolution steps outlined in this document.

SECTION 2: DAMAGED ITEM RECEIVED

If a customer receives a damaged item:

- Report must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery
- Customer must provide clear photographic evidence of the damage and original packaging
- Bazario will review the claim within 2 business days
- Approved claims will receive a full refund or replacement at the customer's choice
- Items do not need to be returned for damage claims unless specifically requested by Bazario

For perishable items, the reporting window is 24 hours from delivery (see Returns and Refunds Policy Section 3).

SECTION 3: INCORRECT ITEM RECEIVED

If a customer receives an item different from what was ordered:

- Report must be submitted within 7 days of delivery
- Customer must provide a photo of the item received and the packing slip
- Bazario will arrange pickup of the incorrect item and dispatch the correct item within 3–5 business days
- If the correct item is out of stock, a full refund will be issued instead
- Customers are not required to bear any cost for incorrect item disputes

SECTION 4: MISSING ITEMS FROM ORDER

If an item is missing from a multi-item order:

- Report must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery
- Customer must provide a photo of the package and all items received
- Bazario will verify against packing records and dispatch the missing item within 3–5 business days
- If the item is out of stock, a proportional refund will be issued

Repeated missing item claims from the same account may be subject to additional verification.

SECTION 5: ITEM SIGNIFICANTLY NOT AS DESCRIBED

If the received item is materially different from the product description or images on Bazario:

- Report must be submitted within 7 days of delivery
- Customer must describe the discrepancy clearly and provide photos
- Bazario will review the product listing and the claim
- If validated, customer will receive a full refund and may keep or return the item at Bazario discretion
- The product listing will be reviewed and corrected or removed if found misleading

SECTION 6: ORDER MARKED DELIVERED BUT NOT RECEIVED

If tracking shows an order as delivered but the customer has not received it:

- Customer must report within 5 days of the marked delivery date
- Bazario will initiate a carrier investigation within 1 business day
- Investigation typically takes 5–7 business days
- If the carrier confirms non-delivery, a full refund or replacement will be issued
- Bazario is not liable for packages delivered to an incorrect address provided by the customer

Customers are advised to check with neighbors, building reception, and nearby pickup points before filing this dispute.

SECTION 7: UNAUTHORIZED OR FRAUDULENT TRANSACTION

If a customer notices a transaction on their Bazario account they did not authorize:

- Report immediately to Bazario support and the payment provider
- Bazario will freeze the affected order and initiate a fraud investigation
- Customers should also change their Bazario account password immediately
- If fraud is confirmed, a full refund will be issued and the fraudulent order cancelled
- Bazario may require identity verification before processing the refund

Customers are responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials. Bazario is not liable for unauthorized transactions resulting from customer negligence.

SECTION 8: MARKETPLACE SELLER DISPUTES

For disputes involving marketplace seller orders:

- Customers must first attempt resolution directly with the seller via Bazario messaging
- If the seller does not respond within 3 business days or refuses a valid claim, the customer may escalate to Bazario
- Bazario will mediate and may issue a refund directly, recovering costs from the seller
- Seller disputes must be escalated within 30 days of delivery

Bazario Buyer Protection (see Returns and Refunds Policy Section 8) covers marketplace orders where the seller is unresponsive or the item is significantly not as described.

SECTION 9: DISPUTE RESOLUTION TIMELINE

Bazario aims to resolve all disputes within the following timelines:

- Damaged / incorrect / missing item: 2–5 business days
- Not as described: 3–7 business days
- Not received (carrier investigation): 7–14 business days
- Fraud investigation: 5–10 business days
- Marketplace seller mediation: 5–10 business days after escalation

Customers will be kept updated via email and app notifications throughout the process.

SECTION 10: DISPUTE ABUSE AND REPEATED CLAIMS

Bazario monitors dispute patterns to detect abuse. Accounts with unusually high dispute rates, repeated claims for the same issue types, or patterns inconsistent with genuine disputes may be subject to:

- Additional verification requirements before claims are processed
- Temporary hold on dispute resolutions pending review
- Account restrictions or suspension in severe cases

Fraudulent dispute claims are a violation of Bazario Terms of Service and may result in permanent account termination and legal action.
